RSVP BUTTON

UPDATE STEPS
1) Edit files locally (index.html or styles.css or script.js).
2) On GitHub, open your repo andreas-evite → Add file → Upload files.
3) Drag in your updated files → Commit changes.
4) Your live site updates within ~1 minute: https://slv1213.github.io/andreas-evite/

COLOR PRESETS
- Open styles.css and choose a palette at the top. Keep the default or uncomment one of the alternatives.

SNOW DENSITY
- In script.js, change FLAKES = 70 to more/less for vibe control.
